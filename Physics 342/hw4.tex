			\begin{proof}
				By Euler's formula, \begin{align*}
					e^{i(u+v)} & = e^{iu}e^{iv} = \cos(u+v) + i \sin(u+v) \\
						& = \left(\cos u + i \sin u\right)\left(\cos v + i \sin v \right) \\
						& = \cos u \cos v + i \cos u \sin v + i \sin u \cos v - \sin u \sin v
